make the dilicious upma

ingredients

ingrediens name quantity
Semolina (Rava) 1 cup
Ghee or Oil 2 tablespoons
Cumin Seeds 1 teaspoon
Asafoetida (Hing) 1/2 teaspoon
Green Chilies (finely chopped) 1-2 teaspoon
Ginger (grated) 1/4 inch
Mixed Vegetable 1/2 cups
Cashew Nuts 10-12
Water 2 cups

process to make

  1. Roast the Semolina: In a pan, dry roast the semolina on low heat until it turns light golden and aromatic. Set it aside.
  2. Prepare the Tempering: In the same pan, heat ghee or oil. Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Once they splutter, add asafoetida.
  3. Add Spices: Add chopped green chilies and grated ginger. Sauté for a minute until fragrant.
  4. Add Lentils and Nuts: Add urad dal, chana dal, and cashew nuts. Sauté until the dals turn golden brown and cashews become slightly crunchy.
  5. Cook Vegetables: Add the mixed vegetables to the pan. Sauté for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Add Water: Pour in 2 cups of water and bring it to a boil. Add salt to taste.
  7. Make the Upma: Once the water is boiling, slowly add the roasted semolina, stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  8. Cook and Stir: Cook the mixture on low heat, stirring occasionally until the semolina absorbs all the water and becomes soft.
  9. Garnish: Turn off the heat and garnish with fresh coriander leaves and a squeeze of lemon juice (optional).
  10. Serve:Serve the upma hot with coconut chutney or pickles.

This recipe is quick and easy, perfect for a healthy breakfast or snack!
