make the dilicious biryani

ingredients

For Marination:

ingrediens name quantity

For Marination:
Chicken (bone-in pieces) 500 grams
Yogurt (curd) 1 cup
Red chili powder 1 teaspoon
Turmeric powder 1/2 teaspoon
Salt 1 teaspoon
Lemon juice 1 tablespoon
For Rice:
Basmati rice 2 cups
Water 6 cups
Bay leaves 2
Cloves 4

process to make

    1. Marinate the Chicken

  • Mix chicken pieces with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, salt, lemon juice, and oil.
  • Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes (or overnight for better flavor).

    • 2. Cook the Rice

  • Rinse and soak basmati rice for 20–30 minutes.
  • Boil water with whole spices (bay leaves, cinnamon, cardamom, and cloves) and salt.
  • Cook the rice until 70-80% done (grains should still be firm). Drain and set aside.

    • 3. Prepare the Masala

  • Heat oil and ghee in a pan; fry sliced onions until golden brown.
  • Add green chilies, chopped tomatoes, and the marinated chicken. Cook until the chicken is tender and the oil separates.
  • Mix in chopped mint and coriander leaves.

    • 4. Layer the Biryani

  • In a large pot, spread a layer of cooked chicken masala at the bottom.
  • Add a layer of partially cooked rice on top. Repeat layers if necessary.
  • Drizzle saffron milk, ghee, fried onions, and rose water or kewra water over the rice.

    • 5. Dum Cooking

  • Cover the pot with a tight lid. Seal with dough or a heavy weight if necessary.
  • Cook on low heat (dum) for 20–25 minutes.
  • Let it rest for 5 minutes before gently mixing and serving.
  • Enjoy your flavorful chicken biryani!
